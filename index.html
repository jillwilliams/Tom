About Us

Tom MacDonald is the largest independent rapper in North America. He went viral with his hit song "Dear Rappers" on August 11, 2017. Tom continues to produce viral videos which he always drops on Fridays, with the cover-art dropping on Thursdays. His girlfriend Nova is the producer of his videos and is also a rapper.
